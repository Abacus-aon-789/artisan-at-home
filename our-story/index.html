· Our Story ·

A leap of faith, a life in food.

Hello! Welcome welcome, you've found us!

Jake and Abi here — the classic duo of Type A hyper-organised and Type B creative, behind Artisan at Home. Partners in business, and in life. We met nearly 8 years ago at a music festival, which is one of our big loves in life. And just as equal (if not a smidge more) was our love of food that brought us together.

The Chef

Meet Jake.

Jake has been a professional Chef for over 20 years — from Gastro Pubs in the UK, to fine dining, vibrant Delis and Corporate R&D in NZ. A story that Jake's parents love to tell, is when at 5 years old he ordered a Lobster chowder off the menu (and yes, his very kind parents obliged!) — so it's safe to say that he was born with a taste for great food!

A true creative at heart, if it wasn't mixing music, or donning a paintbrush, Jake was thinking of new flavour combinations and pushing the boundaries of what an ingredient can do. A mantra he lives by is that everything on the plate needs to have a place and a purpose — whether it's for nutrient density or flavour, it needs to have earnt the right to be there.

The Heart

Meet Abi.

I, Abi, have always been the "foodie" of the group — the go-to for brunch recommendations for as long as I can remember. From pouring through Delia Smith and Rick Stein cooking books on my Dad's bookshelf, to setting up my very own Micro-cakery.

Food is one of those things that every time I taste something beautiful, I do a little happy dance. I have such an appreciation for the art form and skill that goes into creating something aesthetically beautiful, that also tastes magical. And as luck would have it — I ended up with an incredibly talented Chef for a partner!

What Drives Us

The North Star.

Whilst doing some extensive travel around the UK and Europe, we pondered life's big questions. What are we here to do? At some point through Southern France, with a baguette in one hand and Brie de Meaux in the other, it clicked. Our north star has always been incredible food — not just eating it, but creating the soulful, artisan experiences that make people stop and savour the moment.

And so, Artisan at Home was born. Jake brings his years of professional culinary craft to the kitchen as our Private Chef, while I handle the operations and everything in between.

In a world where everything can be manufactured, our focus is on real, honest food: spray-free, no seed oils, and locally sourced. Whether it's an intimate gathering or a larger celebration, we're here to create something effortlessly extraordinary.

— Abi & Jake

How We Cook

Our philosophy

🌿

Spray Free

Clean flavours, grown the right way. We prioritise local, spray-free produce and supporting farmers who genuinely care for the land and food they grow.

📍

Locally Sourced

Wherever possible, we source from New Zealand growers and producers we know and trust. Shorter supply chains mean better flavour and fresher ingredients.

🫒

Zero Seed Oils

Real fats. Real food. Always. We cook exclusively with quality fats — butter, tallow, cold-pressed olive oil, and coconut oil.

🌾

Seasonal Always

Every menu is built around what's at its very best right now. Seasonal cooking means better flavour and better nutrition — which all leads to delicious food.
